Plant profile

Lavender

Fragrant · Sunny · Calming

About this plant

Lavender is a fragrant herb known for its calming aroma and beautiful purple blooms. It thrives in full sunlight and dry, airy soil. Perfect for sunny balconies where it can soak up long hours of light.

It dislikes humidity and overwatering, so it requires a bit of careful attention in India’s monsoon. Well-draining soil and plenty of airflow are essential for keeping this plant healthy.

Light

Needs 5–7 hours of sunlight daily. Full sun is ideal.

Water

Water sparingly. Let soil dry completely between waterings.

Soil

Sandy, well-draining soil; cactus mix + perlite works great.

Pet safety

Safe in small amounts; avoid large pet ingestion.

Seasonal behavior

  • Summer: Loves bright sunlight and heat; grows well.
  • Monsoon: Very sensitive; reduce watering heavily.
  • Winter: Mild climates help flowering; protect from frost.

Quick info

  • Scientific name: Lavandula
  • Type: Mediterranean herb
  • Care level: Medium
  • Ideal spot: Bright balcony with airflow
  • Growth speed: Slow
  • Height: 1–3 feet
