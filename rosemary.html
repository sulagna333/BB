Plant profile

Rosemary

Herb · Full sun · Balcony

About this plant

Rosemary is a fragrant Mediterranean herb used in cooking and loved for its woody stems and needle-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it perfect for balconies in warm, bright climates.

It hates overwatering but loves strong light and light breezes. A healthy rosemary bush can grow for years and provide fresh sprigs for your pasta, potatoes, breads, and herbal teas.

Light

6+ hours of strong sunlight. Needs full sun daily.

Water

Water only when the topsoil dries. Very sensitive to overwatering.

Soil

Dry, well-draining soil (cactus mix + compost works well).

Pet safety

Safe. Edible herb.

Seasonal behavior

  • Summer: Loves the heat and grows fastest.
  • Monsoon: Reduce watering significantly; avoid waterlogged soil.
  • Winter: Growth slows; ensure it still gets 4–6 hours of light.

Quick info

  • Scientific name: Salvia rosmarinus
  • Type: Woody herb
  • Care level: Medium
  • Ideal spot: South-facing balcony or window
  • Growth speed: Medium
  • Height: 1–4 feet
