Plant profile

Snake Plant

Indoor · Low light · Very hardy

About this plant

Snake Plant (Sansevieria) is one of the toughest indoor plants you can own — it survives low light, irregular watering, and neglect. Ideal for hostels, apartments, or busy people who forget to water.

It has upright sword-like leaves and grows slowly but steadily. It also improves air quality and is often recommended for bedrooms and study rooms.

Light

Low to medium light; tolerates corners where sunlight is minimal.

Water

Water only when soil is fully dry. Overwatering kills it.

Soil

Well-draining mix. Add extra perlite or sand for airflow.

Pet safety

Not pet-safe. Keep away from chewing pets.

Seasonal behavior

  • Summer: Water slightly more often; grows faster.
  • Monsoon: Be careful — soil stays wet longer.
  • Winter: Water very rarely; once every 2–3 weeks.

Quick info

  • Scientific name: Sansevieria trifasciata
  • Type: Indoor hardy plant
  • Care level: Very easy
  • Ideal spot: Corners, bedroom, study desk
  • Growth speed: Slow
  • Height: 1–3 feet indoors
