Plant profile

Adenium (Desert Rose)

Succulent · Balcony · Sun-loving

About this plant

Adenium, commonly known as Desert Rose, is a stunning succulent with a thick caudex (bulb-like trunk) and vibrant flowers. It loves bright sunlight and thrives in hot, dry climates.

Perfect for sunny balconies, this plant rewards good light with beautiful blooms in shades of pink, red, or white. But it is extremely sensitive to overwatering — the roots rot quickly if soil stays wet.

Light

Needs strong direct sun for at least 4–6 hours daily.

Water

Water once soil is bone dry. Overwatering causes root rot fast.

Soil

Cactus/succulent mix with lots of sand or perlite.

Pet safety

Not pet-safe. Sap is toxic if ingested.

Seasonal behavior

  • Summer: Peak growth + flowering season.
  • Monsoon: Water very rarely; soil stays wet longer.
  • Winter: Enters dormancy; leaves may fall; water extremely little.

Quick info

  • Scientific name: Adenium obesum
  • Type: Succulent shrub
  • Care level: Medium
  • Ideal spot: Sunny balcony / terrace
  • Growth speed: Medium
  • Height: 1–3 feet
