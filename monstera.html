Plant profile

Monstera Deliciosa

Indoor · Tropical · Statement leaves

About this plant

Monstera Deliciosa is a fast-growing tropical plant known for its big, split leaves. It climbs in nature, using trees for support, and can be trained on a moss pole or stake indoors.

This plant loves bright, indirect light and slightly moist soil. If you give it enough space and support, it will reward you with dramatic new leaves that unfurl with fresh holes and splits.

Light

Bright, indirect light. Avoid direct harsh afternoon sun.

Water

Water once the top 2–3 cm of soil feels dry to touch.

Soil

Well-draining mix: cocopeat, perlite, and compost.

Pet safety

Not pet safe. Keep away from chewing pets.

Seasonal behavior

  • Summer: Fastest growth; loves humidity.
  • Monsoon: Reduce watering slightly to avoid soggy soil.
  • Winter: Growth slows down; keep away from cold drafts.

Quick info

  • Scientific name: Monstera deliciosa
  • Type: Indoor tropical
  • Care level: Medium
  • Ideal spot: Bright living room corner
  • Growth speed: Fast
  • Height: 2–7 feet indoors
